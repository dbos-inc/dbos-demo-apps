Build reliable software effortlessly

DBOS adds durable execution to your applications, checkpointing their workflows and steps to Postgres. If your apps ever crash, DBOS recovers them from their last completed steps.

Try it: launch a workflow, then crash this application. When you restart, the workflow always resumes from exactly where it left off.

app/main.py
