Durable Workflow Demo

Build apps that are resilient to any failure.

Add @DBOS.workflow() and @DBOS.step() decorators to your code. DBOS durably executes your program, checkpointing each step to Postgres. If your app ever crashes, DBOS recovers it from the last completed step.

Try it: launch a workflow, then crash the app mid-run. When it restarts, the workflow always resumes exactly where it left off.

app/main.py
